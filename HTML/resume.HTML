My Life: Here is a quick recap of my life.

  1. Childhood
    • I was born on November 15th, 2000, in Walnut Creek, California
    • I lived in Walnut Creek until I was 6 and enjoyed playing with trains and hot wheels
    • After about 4 years my little sister Ali was born and life got exciting
    • We moved to Issaquah, Washington, where my two other little siblings were born, Emmy and Drew.
    • I went to Elementary School at Grand Ridge and Middle School at Pacific Cascade.
    • I picked up basktetball, soccer and baseball while I was there
    • In the summer of 2015 we moved to Pleasanton California
  2. Teenage Years
    • I attended Amador Valley Highschool
    • I played on the basketball team and was track Captain while participating in the 400 meter race and High Jump.
    • I spent my time exploring the amazing Northern California countryside surrounding me, often finding hikes and camping sites nearby.
    • I gained an interest in cliff jumping and river rafting while I was here.
    • I spent almost every day with my friends.
    • I submitted my mission papers and recieved my call to go to the Pachuca Mexico Mission!
    • Finished up my last summer before my mission buiulding a school in Fiji through HEFY.
  3. Mission and College
    • I left for my mission on August 13th, 2019
    • I met amazing people as I served in just two areas in Pachuca: Pitahayas and Presas.
    • Around March 2021 we received news that COVID-19 was spreading worldwide and for our saftey the church decided to send the foregin missionaries back home.
    • I was home for 5 months before being reassigned to the Boston, Massachussetts mission where I stayed for 7 months before being sent back to Mexico to finish.
    • Upon finishing my mission I started going to BYU in Provo, majoring in Finance.
    • I started dating my amazing girlfriend, Eden, on January 23rd, 2022, and it has been an great 10 months.
    • I live with my best friends and spend my time playing sports, going to the gym and eating good food.
